About National Star Investment Co. Ltd

Founded in 2013, National Star Investment Co. Ltd is a reputable and dynamic general trading company based in South Sudan. We specialize in the trading of goods and services across a range of sectors, delivering high-quality products and reliable services to our clients. We are known for our dedication to customer satisfaction, competitive pricing, and on-time delivery.

Vision

Perceiving customer needs at the highest level by providing them flexibility in sourcing, competitive prices, on-time deliveries, and customized solutions tailored to their unique needs.

Mission

Our mission is to be a South Sudanese company recognized nationwide for excellence, consistently exceeding customer expectations with quality services and products.
